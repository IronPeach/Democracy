Athen System:

Athen sytem is a system that choose people via a random system - sortition, which is kinda like people form a lottery. They will choose from their citizen volunteers, that has to be adult, male, and native, which is not a lot of their population.

This system was originally created because of the contant conflict between the nobles and the elites, the people are not having powers, while the elites are having most of them.

The lucky citizens that are choosed will go to Ecclesia. Once seatted, a 500 people council - the boule, will lead the concerstaion and explain the current topic. Once they has set the goal, the voters will discuss and vote by raising their hands.

Afther the vote, the votes will be accounted for and the order will be executed, and the voters tht attended the meeting will be paid for their participation.

American System:

In American system, all citizens that is over 18 years old can vote, there is no limitatons based on a person's identity.

The American government system is originally created right after people escape from the rules of a monarch, and people desire freedom, and do not wish for the power goes into the hands of one person, or a group.

In United States, citizens are elected though a system called Electorial Colledge. In the systems, citizens elect the representative of their state to elect for the president, a representative democracy.

During the voting, Americans are expected to go to a voting booth that is set up national wide. The ballot they put in the election will elect representatives that will represent them to vote for a new leader.

Similarities

  • Both included people's opinion as a direction
  • Only adult citizens are allowed to vote
  • Both is created to go against power being collected into a certain person or a group, preventing despotisms, autoracy, or dictatorship.

Diffrences

  • Athen system use a direct democracy where people can vote directly, and their vote will have a direct impact on the final outcome, while the system in United States use a representative democracy(electorial colledge), where people vote for a representative that represent them to vote.
  • Athen system use sortation to pick voters, meaning only certain of the qualified people can have a voice to express their opinion, and it is chosen completely by random. While American voting system accept all qualified people to vote.
  • All the voting in Athen takes place in the Ecclesia, while American set up booth national wide for people to vote in.
